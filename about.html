About Me

Professional Background

Current Role

I am currently serving as a Computer Science teacher at St. Xavier's School, Godavari, Lalitpur, Nepal. With a passion for technology education, I work towards preparing students with strong programming fundamentals and problem-solving skills essential for the digital age.

Teaching Philosophy

My approach to computer science education is based on the following principles:

  • Making complex programming concepts accessible and engaging
  • Encouraging hands-on learning through coding projects
  • Developing problem-solving and computational thinking skills
  • Fostering collaboration and teamwork in technical environments
  • Staying updated with emerging technologies and best practices
  • Supporting students in their journey towards tech careers
